What is this project about?

ETHDonuts is a simple showcase of my talents. I am a full stack engineer with over 10 years experience (closer to 20) working in professional settings and 5 years developing crypto projects.

ETHDonut hodlers will be able to enter a private community, which will get amazing insights on how projects are launched, be able to participate and give feedback and input, ETHDonut hodlers are entitled to FREE NFTs/ERC20s from all projects, and a good amount of them too. Diamond hands will get rewarded more than softies.

So? What makes you special?

To put a long story short, it took me 10 hours to learn the ERC-721 standard, research the proper methods for layering images (I have not seen a single project using SVG, I do not know why - but this is really silly.), and to build this website with the full web3 implementation and solidity contract.

By myself.

Why should we care?

Because I'm bored and work already 16+ hours a day building things that I usually never launch.

I might as well build what ETHDonut hodlers want me to build, and to launch them in a proper fashion as well.

Why don't you launch these projects yourself?

I can, and I have. I've also worked with many teams. I enjoy meeting new people, learning new skills, and teaching new skills to people. What I envision here is a core group of ETHDonut hodlers who are passionate about what we launch, as they're the ones guiding the process.

This eases my burden of creating communities for these projects, which is why I choose to launch very few projects, as I do not need to do it for money. I like to develop, and I believe this project may allow me to do so.

Why don't we get other people to build what we want?

You sure can. But, considering how quickly I work, my wide skillset, my team of marketers, artists and a few developers, it's sure that it won't be completed as quickly with the good chance it wasn't built properly.

I've built many contracts still in use today in the ERC20 DeFi world, and worked on some large projects. What I do not know how to do, my first step is to research teh subject. I am not shy to find the experts and ask the questions if needed. There are very few roadblocks for me and what I can achieve, even less with an army of 500 passionate ETHDonut hodlers.
